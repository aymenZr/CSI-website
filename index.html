Scientific Club of INPTIC

The Scientific Club of the National Institute of Post and Information and Communication Technology is a technical club run by a group of volunteer students under the supervision of the Institute’s administration. It was established in 2009, in which various activities are carried out that will communicate information and knowledge in various technical fields such as programming, electronic design, networks and cyber security, which Depends on participation, creativity and innovation under the slogan
